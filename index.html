About Me

I'm Gyau Boahen Elvis, a first year student offering Computer Engineering at the Kwame Nkrumah University Of Science and Technology. I come from Kookusua Number 1, a small village in the Bono region. I had my Primary and Junior High School education at the Fountain of life School and Institute of Business Studies both located in the Western region of Ghana, West Africa. It was after my junior high school education that I joined the Arson Innovate Tech Kids. I wrote my first "hello world" using html and after then I fell in love with coding. Seeing what what a small piece of code could do always marvelled my mind and it made me think what next. I had my senior high school education at the St. Augustine's College where I offered General Science.
